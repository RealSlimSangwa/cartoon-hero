Danny Phantom

About Danny

Danny Fenton Aka Danny Phantom is a half-human, half-ghost, superhero. As a human he is a regular teenager facing the difficulties of high school but with the help of his friends and family he uses his ghost powers to protect the human world from evil ghosts that come from the ghost world.
